# HHI Governance Audit — Offer

## What This Is
A formal governance audit based on HHI_GOV_01, focused on decision authority,
escalation integrity, and behavioral drift over time.

## What We Assess
- Who can stop the system
- Whether stop power is enforceable at runtime
- Escalation integrity over repeated use
- Evidence persistence and auditability

## Deliverables
- Governance Scorecard (0–10)
- Enterprise Audit Report (PDF)
- Risk classification (Non-governed / Soft / Audit-ready)
- Written remediation guidance

## Timeline
- Intake: 1–2 days
- Audit: 5 business days
- Delivery: PDF + call

## Price
$5,000 per system (single deployment)

Governance is infrastructure. This audit evaluates whether it exists.
